yield: 8

cookTime: 45

ingredients:
- 6 slices of bread, sliced into sticks
- 2 eggs
- 1 mashed banana
- ½ cups milk
- ½ tsp cinnamon
- ½ tsp vanilla

directions:
- Mix eggs banana milk cinnamon and vanilla.
- Dip bread,
- Put on lined baking sheet.
- Bake at 350 for 20 minutes. Flip at 10 minutes.
