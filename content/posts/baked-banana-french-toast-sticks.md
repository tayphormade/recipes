---

##### Ingredients
- 6 slices of bread, sliced into sticks
- 2 eggs
- 1 mashed banana
- ½ cups milk
- ½ tsp cinnamon
- ½ tsp vanilla

##### Directions
1. Mix eggs banana milk cinnamon and vanilla.
1. Dip bread,
1. Put on lined baking sheet.
1. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes. Flip at 10 minutes.
